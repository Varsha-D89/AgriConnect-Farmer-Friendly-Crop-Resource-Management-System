Farming Tips

Expert Tips for Better Yield

Simple, practical tips that every farmer should know to maximize productivity.

1

Test Your Soil Regularly

Soil testing every 2 years helps identify nutrient deficiencies and choose the right fertilizers.

2

Use Drip Irrigation

Drip irrigation saves up to 50% water while delivering moisture directly to plant roots.

3

Practice Crop Rotation

Rotating crops each season improves soil health, reduces pests, and increases overall yield.

4

Monitor Crop Disease Early

Inspect fields weekly for signs of disease or pests. Early detection saves crops and costs.

5

Apply Organic Compost

Adding compost improves soil structure, water retention, and provides natural nutrients.

6

Time Your Sowing Correctly

Sowing at the right time based on monsoon onset dramatically improves germination rates.

7

Keep Records of Expenses

Tracking input costs helps identify wastage and improve profitability each season.

8

Use Certified Seeds

Certified seeds have higher germination rates, disease resistance, and better yield potential.
