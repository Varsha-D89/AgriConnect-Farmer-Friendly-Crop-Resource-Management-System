🌾 Rice – Harvesting

Harvest when 80-85% of grains turn golden. Drain field 10 days before harvest. Moisture content should be 20-22%. Thresh promptly to avoid losses.

🌿 Wheat – Sowing

Sow wheat seeds at 100-125 kg/ha in rows 22.5cm apart. Ideal soil temperature 20-25°C. Varieties: HD-2967, GW-322 for north India.

🌿 Wheat – Crown Root Irrigation

First irrigation (Crown Root Initiation) at 20-25 days. Critical stage — never miss. Apply 60kg N/ha with this irrigation for best results.

🌿 Wheat – Fertilizer Top Dressing

🌿 Wheat – Harvesting

Harvest at physiological maturity (grain hard, golden). Use combine harvester. Target moisture 12-14% for storage. Expected yield: 45-55 quintals/ha.

Harvest at cob maturity (husk dry, silks brown). Dry cobs to 12% moisture before shelling. Expected yield: 35-45 quintals/ha with hybrid varieties.
