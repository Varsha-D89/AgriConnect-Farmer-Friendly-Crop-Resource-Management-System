Soil Guide

Know Your Soil Type

🏔️

Clay Soil

Heavy, holds water well. Best for rice, sugarcane, wheat. Poor drainage.

🌍

Loamy Soil

Ideal balance of sand, silt, clay. Best for most crops. Excellent fertility.

🏜️

Sandy Soil

Light, drains fast. Good for groundnut, bajra. Needs frequent watering.

⬛

Black Cotton Soil

Rich in minerals, expands when wet. Best for cotton, soybean, sorghum.

🔴

Red Laterite Soil

Iron-rich, low in humus. Best for ragi, groundnut, millets.
