Disease Guide

Common Crop Diseases

🍂

Leaf Blight

Water-soaked spots turning brown. Spreads rapidly in humid conditions.

Treatment: Mancozeb spray + remove infected leaves

⬜

Powdery Mildew

White powdery coating on leaves. Common in dry weather with cool nights.

Treatment: Sulfur spray + reduce humidity + improve air flow

🟡

Yellow Mosaic Virus

Yellow-green mosaic pattern on leaves. Transmitted by whiteflies.

Treatment: Control whiteflies with Imidacloprid + remove infected plants

🟤

Root Rot

Roots turn brown/black and decay. Caused by waterlogging and fungal infection.

Treatment: Improve drainage + Trichoderma bio-fungicide soil drench

🟠

Rust Disease

Orange/rust-colored pustules on leaves. Spreads rapidly in cool, moist weather.

Treatment: Propiconazole + Tebuconazole fungicide spray

📸 Photo Tips

  • ✅ Take photos in natural daylight
  • ✅ Capture both sides of the leaf
  • ✅ Include stem and roots if affected
  • ✅ Use clean lens — wipe before clicking
  • ❌ Avoid blurry or dark images
  • ❌ Don't include non-plant objects
