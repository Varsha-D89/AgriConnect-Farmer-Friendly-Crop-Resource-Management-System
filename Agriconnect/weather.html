Seasonal Guide

Weather & Farming Calendar

☀️

Summer (Mar–May)

High temperatures 35–45°C. Ideal for summer vegetables, watermelon, and muskmelon. Increase irrigation frequency.

🌧️

Kharif / Monsoon (Jun–Oct)

Heavy rainfall 600–1500mm. Perfect for rice, maize, cotton, sugarcane. Ensure proper drainage.

❄️

Winter / Rabi (Nov–Feb)

Temperatures 10–25°C. Best season for wheat, mustard, gram, vegetables. Light irrigation needed.

🌸

Pre-Monsoon (Apr–Jun)

Variable weather with occasional rains. Good for preparing fields, sowing nurseries, and soil amendment.
